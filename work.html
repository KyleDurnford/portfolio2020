Real Estate App

An app that helps users find homes near them using a tinder-like concept. Users have the ability to swipe on homes that appear in their feed to either like or dislike them. If a home is liked, it is added to a list that can be re-ordered and ranked. Users also have the ability to tap on the home to see more information about it. All home information is pulled from a national feed that gets updated daily. Users must first go through a sign up process that asks for their preferences to tailor the home search to them. All information is gathered and sent to a database. Once enough data is saved, machine learning steps in to help the user find better homes for them.

One Man's Treasure

A complete website revamp for a local antique store. The original website was dark and did not suit the creative, friendly and community driven atmosphere that One Man's Treasure has created. In an effort to change this, brighter colors were brought in that represented the shop better. The creative grid layout reflects the fun and interesting interior layout of the shop. The images show a wide range of antiques that can be found in the store. The website now looks better, loads faster and is completely WCAG 2.1 compliant to allow the vast age range of customers to easily explore the website.

Esports Logo

A logo done in the esports style for a twitch streamer. The goal was to create a logo that stands out among other esports logos while still having some of the same familiarity. Instead of the entire body of a snake that most logos have, just the eye is used. The bright colors on top of the darker background make the eye stand out. The typeface is rounded and sharp to imitate a snake. Gradients and two-tones are use throughout to make the logo seem more alive.
